60/40 Indica Dominant Hybrid 


Black Truffle is an exotic strain renowned for its potent effects, captivating aroma, and distinctive flavor profile. It delivers a powerful yet balanced experience, inducing a euphoric uplift followed by deep relaxation. When smoked or vaporized, it delights the palate with a complex blend of nutty, woody, and spicy flavors, leaving a lingering sweetness on the exhale. Overall, Black Truffle offers a luxurious sensory journey cherished by cannabis connoisseurs seeking a sophisticated and rewarding cannabis experience.
