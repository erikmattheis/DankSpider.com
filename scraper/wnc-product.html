60/40 Sativa Dominant Hybrid

Tiny OG a meticulously crafted hybrid strain born from the union of two legendary parents - Tiny Bomb and OG. This harmonious blend brings together the best of both worlds, resulting in a truly exceptional cannabis cultivar. The fragrance of Tiny OG is a symphony of earthy undertones complemented by pungent skunkiness. Hints of citrus and pine add a zesty freshness that dances through the air. Upon closer inspection, subtle floral notes emerge, creating a complex and inviting olfactory experience.Upon inhalation, Tiny OG offers an exhilarating burst of citrus zest followed by a deep, earthy foundation. As the smoke or vapor swirls over the palate, a delicate sweetness emerges, reminiscent of fresh pine. The aftertaste is marked by a subtle spiciness, leaving a satisfying and lingering impression. This strain possesses a well-balanced combination of effects, ensuring a delightful experience for both recreational and medicinal users. The initial onset is characterized by a gentle cerebral euphoria, inducing a sense of blissful relaxation without overwhelming the mind. A calming wave gradually spreads throughout the body, alleviating tension and stress. If you are sensitive to THC, please do not purchase this product, use with caution! Please be responsible with this product and consume in the comfort of your home. Do not drive or operate heavy machinery while using this product. This product is best used when mixed with CBD for a full on entourage effect and is very powerful. Please check your state and local laws before purchasing high THCa hemp flower. Buds range in size from medium to small and are slowly cold cured for the perfect cure and moisture content. Tiny OG tests high in Caryophyllene, Linalool, and Myrcene.
