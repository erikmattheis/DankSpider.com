60/40 Sativa Dominant Hybrid

Snow Man presents a compelling option for those seeking a strain that marries a thoughtful mental journey with a touch of physical ease. Its unique flavor profile and robust effects make it a noteworthy choice for seasoned cannabis enthusiasts seeking therapeutic relief. This strain offers a high that's tailor-made for leisurely afternoons when you're seeking a tranquil escape into contemplation. From the onset, you'll experience a surge of happiness and elevation, accompanied by a subtle surge of creative energy that inspires deep reflection on past, present, and future. This introspective journey is complemented by a gentle touch of physical relaxation, grounding you in the present moment without inducing sedation. Snow Man's flavor profile is characterized by a bold combination of spicy diesel notes, harmoniously interwoven with a delicate hint of sweet vanilla that lingers on the palate. The aroma mirrors this complexity, offering a rich blend of earthy diesel and piquant kush, with an herbal undertone that acquires a tangy edge when the buds are ignited. If you are sensitive to THC, please do not purchase this product, use with caution! Please be responsible with this product and consume in the comfort of your home. Do not drive or operate heavy machinery while using this product. This product is best used when mixed with CBD for a full on entourage effect and is very powerful. Please check your state and local laws before purchasing high THCa hemp flower. Buds range in size from medium to small and are slowly cold cured for the perfect cure and moisture content.
